Painters I like

Up and coming

I saw this artist in New York
outside of the Met.

  artist name: Oliver Kufalo

His artwork has characterisitcs as follows

  1. Ballerinas dancing in a spotlight
  2. A 3D aspect via thick paint coming forward off the canvas
  3. Occasionally colors
    due to having a "colorful" spotlight instead of just white

I like Kufalo's work for several reasons

  • The effect of the illuminated dancers against a black backdrop creates a dramatic visual experience
  • His use of texture and light work together well
  • Edgar Degas is one of my favorite artists
    -- and this seems to be a modern take on Degas' paintings created to capture the physicality of dancer
    -- although Kufalo's work is much more consistent in its direct frontal vantage point and has a very different style of painting
